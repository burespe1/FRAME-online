Terminator Law Enforcement Agency

Overview

This Terminator shall represent an Authority taking the necessary measures or actions to achieve compliance with laws, rules and regulations for the management of road traffic.  If any violations of laws are detected by the System, the terminator shall provide sufficient data for the Authority to identify and initiate prosecution of the offenders.  This data that is provided by the System shall as a minimum comprise such things as, visual image, vehicle identity, location, time, date, nature of violation.
