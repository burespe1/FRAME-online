Dataflow
mt_inter-urban_lane_status


Description

  It contains details of the current lane use setting that have been imposed on one or more parts of the inter-urban road network.  The details may include but not be limited to the identity and/or location of the lane(s), types of vehicle that are prohibited (or allowed) and the direction of travel.
