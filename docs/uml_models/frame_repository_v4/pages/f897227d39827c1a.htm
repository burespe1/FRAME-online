Dataflow
tors.utms-urban_traffic_management_strategies


Description

It contains details of the new urban traffic management strategy or special vehicle priority route that is just being implemented by the system that should be of interest to a geographically adjacent (or relevant) urban traffic management system. The details will comprise such things as the affected junctions, method of control, actual junction timings, reason for change and previous strategy.
