Low Level Function
8.2.2.2.3 Process on-board Payments

Overview

This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:

(1) The ability to receive and process data about payments that have been made by the Freight Vehicle Driver.
(2) The ability to load information about these payments into the store of Resources Data.
(3) The ability to regularly extract the payment data from the store of Resources Data, process them for reimbursement and replace them in the store of Resources Data.

Functional Requirements

(a) receive receipt from driver and store them in the store of Resources Data
(b) on a regular time basis
(b1) extract all receipt data from data base,
(b2) compute them in order to prepare them for reimbursement operations,
(b3) store results in the database.
