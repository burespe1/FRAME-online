Overview

This High-level Function shall provide facilities for the global management of the fleet resources including vehicles, equipment and drivers.  Facilities for resource management during trip are excluded from this Function.  These facilities shall be found in the Manage Driver, Vehicle, Equipment and Cargo During Trip Function (8.3).  The Function shall consist of the following lower level Functions:
