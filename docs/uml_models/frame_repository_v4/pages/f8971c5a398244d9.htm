Dataflow
mt_long_term_maintenance_data


Description

It contains data that is being transferred from the maintenance management functions to those for incident management. This data provides information on where and when long term maintenance road works are occurring and is to be used in the preparation of incident management strategies. These will minimise the impact of the road-works on the flow of traffic through the road network.
