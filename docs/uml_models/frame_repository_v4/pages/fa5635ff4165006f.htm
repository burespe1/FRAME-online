Dataflow
fesp.gip-poi_information


Description

It contains information, such as their location, opening times, price of service, nearest transport service points, access information, etc. about "Points of Interest" (e.g. monuments, museums, parks, gardens, etc.) in a specific locality.  The arrival of the Data Flow may be as a result of a previous request, or it may be unsolicited.
