Dataflow
ftrfc-freight_vehicle_identity_for_zones


Description

It contains a manifestation of the presence of a Freight Vehicle from which its identity can be determined as it approached a loading or unloading zone, or a holding zone to which the Freight Vehicle is being directed because the zone is not available at the requested date/time, or duration.
