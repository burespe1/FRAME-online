List of Datastores


Definition of Datastore

The Functional Areas or the High Level Functions may include Data Stores. They are used to hold data that is used by several High Level Functions within an Area, or the Low Level Functions within a High Level Function. They are also named and numbered. The name is used to indicate what type of data the Store contains. The number consists of two digits, the first being the Area number. The second digit is obtained by numbering from one (1) the Stores in each Functional Area.
