Dataflow
mt.pshvs_forecast_traffic_to_monitor_vehicle_trip


Description

It contains data about predicted unusual traffic conditions within the total road network predicted by the results from traffic simulations and is for use in the monitoring of a trip being implemented by a Driver using an In-vehicle device. The causes of these conditions may include such things as expected road works and road blocks. In both cases the predicted travel times may exceed normal times.
