Dataflow
mt_operator_inter-urban_management_request


Description

It contains requests form the Operator for actions to be performed by the provide inter-urban traffic management Function. These requests comprise but are not limited to such things as the imposition of inter-urban traffic management strategies, and the output of the "log" containing details of previous inter-urban traffic management strategy implementations.
