Terminator Principal
 Diagram   Definition    TabMemoryProcess

Overview

This Actor within the Consigner/Consignee Terminator shall represent a human entity or organisation that is the originator of a freight request.  The actor may, after a period of negotiation, establish a contract for a freight service with a freight haulage company.  After successful delivery of the Consignment the actor pays the company.
