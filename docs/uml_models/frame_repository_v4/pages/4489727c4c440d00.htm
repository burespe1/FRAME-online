Terminator Tunnel Operator

Overview

This Actor within the Operator Terminator shall represent the human entity that is managing the operation of one or more tunnels in the road network served by the Systems.  This actor includes both the Tunnel Operator who is the user of the System and the System Operator who is its guardian.  The Tunnel Operator will use the System to manage the flow of traffic through the tunnel(s), whilst the System Operator will control the way in which the System manages tunnel(s).  The system may be in communication with more than one human entity that is a Tunnel Operator.  Each entity may belong to the same organisation, or to different organisations and may be responsible for one or more tunnels in the road network.
