Dataflow
td-urban_traffic_enforcement_messages


Description

It contains the manifestation of the output of details of violators of traffic management commands to drivers of all types of vehicles using the urban road network.  It is intended to act as a deterrent to future violations and/or give notice of impending possible prosecution of the driver for the violation.
