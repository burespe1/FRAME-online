Terminator Freight Shipper
 Diagram   Definition    TabMemoryProcess

Overview

This Actor within the Consigner/Consignee Terminator shall represent a human entity or organisation that is a sender and/or recipient of goods and the owner of the details regarding the goods.  It shall interface with the System so that good may be prepared and accepted for transport.
