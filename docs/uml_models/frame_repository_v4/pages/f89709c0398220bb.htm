Low Level Function
4.3.2 Provide PT Maintenance Co-ordination

Overview

This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:

(1) The ability to optimise maintenance schedules based on relevant criteria.
(2) The ability to include optimisation criteria such as faults reported for the PT infrastructure and Vehicles, as well as the current PT service plans and the maintenance plans of other PT operations.

Functional Requirements

(a) continuously monitor for the receipt of the faulty equipment data flow
(b) collect the details of the current planned services and the current maintenance plans of other systems in the relevant data flows
(c) prepare a plan for maintenance intervention co-ordinated with other maintenance activities
(d) communicate the plans to other functions and to the maintenance organisation
(e) when notification is received that the repairs have been completed, send the appropriate data flow.
