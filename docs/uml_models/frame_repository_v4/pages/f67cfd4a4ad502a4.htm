Dataflow
fmms.mmc-urban_crossing_information


Description

It contains warning messages for Drivers, Cyclists and Pedestrians not to enter a multi-modal crossing because either it is about to closed to road traffic, or has closed but is a short distance ahead, and will stop or restrict the normal flow of road traffic through out the urban road network.
