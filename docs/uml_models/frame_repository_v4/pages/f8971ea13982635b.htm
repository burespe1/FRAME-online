Dataflow
mffo.ptja_hazardous_goods_monitoring_information


Description

It contains information that allows the monitoring of hazardous goods movements.  The data flow relates to vehicle position, vehicle and cargo characteristics.  It may be sent either from the ground or from the on-board part of the Manage Freight and Fleet Operations Area.
