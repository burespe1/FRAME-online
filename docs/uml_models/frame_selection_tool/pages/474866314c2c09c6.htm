Dataflow
td-host_vehicle_driver_warning_messages


Description

It contains a warning for the Driver of a Vehicle (the identity of which is included) about potentially unsafe conditions ahead, including a prediction that its forward trajectory will potentially bring it into conflict with something it its path, such as another Vehicle, another Road User, a Pedestrian or a Vulnerable Road User (VRU) and that a particular action should be taken, e.g. slow down, change lanes, plus information about road surface conditions detected by Vehicles that are ahead.
