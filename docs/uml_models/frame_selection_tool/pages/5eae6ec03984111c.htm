Vehicle

This is a Terminator diagram which provides a description of the Terminator breakdown structure into Actors.

Overview

This Terminator shall represent the Vehicle in terms of any functionality that it might contain the main purpose of which is the provision of ITS services.  It consists of the following Actors which represent the different types of Vehicle that can use the road network managed by the System:
Emergency Vehicle
Freight Vehicle
Hazardous Goods Vehicle
Hazardous Goods Vehicle
On-Demand Service Vehicle
Other Vehicle
Private Vehicle
Public Transport Touring Vehicle
Public Transport Vehicle
Vehicle Systems
Each of these Actors shall have interfaces to ITS related functionality in other areas and be able to collect and/or create ITS related data.  The data shall be collected by sensors within the system and shall be limited to those that sense data about the road network in which the Vehicle is operating and those that monitor the condition of the Driver.  The "Vehicle Systems" Actor shall have read only access to data provided by the Vehicle systems".  It and the other Actors shall not be able to control the Vehicle.
