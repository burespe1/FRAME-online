Dataflow
td-urban_traffic_commands and warnings


Description

It contains the manifestation of stop or go (s&g) commands, messages, warnings, lane use and speed commands and details of violators (Vehicle identity) of traffic management commands that is being output to Drivers of all types of vehicles using the urban road network.  The first three of these manifestations will direct the Drivers to take actions so that their progress will make best use of the urban travel network.  The details of violators are intended to act as a deterent to future violations and/or give notice that the Driver of the Vehicle committing the violation may face prosecution in the future.
