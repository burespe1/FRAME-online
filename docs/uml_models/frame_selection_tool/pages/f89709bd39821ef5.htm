Low Level Function
1.4.3 Inform Operators on Transactions

Overview

This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:

(1) An interface that enables for the Operators, and Service Providers to view all the transactions concerning the services that they provide.
(2) The ability for the interface to only provide information to Operators and External Service Providers on request.
(3) The interface shall only have the ability to provide information to External Service Providers that is relevant to them and not information that is relevant to other Service Providers.

Functional Requirements

(a) get the request from the Operator or Service Provider, including its ID, services of interest, period, ...;
(b) extract the corresponding transactions from the store of "transactions" Data, restricting retrieval to only these transactions are those concerning directly the requesting Operator or Service Provider, or those that resulted in some revenue for them;
(c) process them to obtain the desired information;
(d) present the results to the Operator or Service Provider.
