Overview

This Actor within the Vehicle Terminator shall represent a vehicle that is carrying hazardous goods. The Vehicle must be designed and licensed for the purpose of carrying a particular type of hazardous goods. The "hazard" may relate to the type of goods, such as chemicals, combustible or nuclear materials, etc, or to its physical characteristics, e.g. size, weight.
