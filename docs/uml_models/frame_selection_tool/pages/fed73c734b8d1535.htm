Terminator Roadside Equipment
 Diagram   Definition    TabMemoryProcess

Overview

This Terminator shall represent entities that are outside vehicles that are capable of providing information directly to their drivers.  It shall be possible for the information to comprise regulations, commands for all or particular types of Vehicle to use or not use specific lanes in particular segments of the road network, commands providing the maximum vehicle speed, warnings about anything that might affect the flow of traffic, and general information.
