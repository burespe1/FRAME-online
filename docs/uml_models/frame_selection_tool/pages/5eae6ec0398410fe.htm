Terminator Road Pavement
 Diagram   Definition    TabMemoryProcess

Overview

This Terminator shall represent the material that makes up the surface of the road.  The data that is collected shall enable the System to decide what maintenance operations are necessary to ensure that the surface causes no hazard to vehicles, pedestrians, as well as those in wheelchairs, or have imperfect sight.  The different status of the road surfacing that can be measured shall include but not be limited to conditions such as ice, flood water, landslides, etc.
