Terminator Vehicle Renting Agency

Overview

This Actor within the External Service Provider Terminator shall represent an organisation from which it shall be possible to hire a vehicle for part of a trip.  The definition of a vehicle shall comprise but not be limited to a car, coach (for parties), bicycle, taxi, aeroplane, train, or boat.
