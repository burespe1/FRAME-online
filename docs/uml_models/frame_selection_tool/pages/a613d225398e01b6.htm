Terminator Traveller Information Operator
 Diagram   Definition    TabMemoryProcess

Overview

This Actor within the Operator Terminator shall represent a human entity that uses the facilities of the system to provide traveller information.  The system may be in communication with more than one human entity that is a Traveller Information Operator.  Each entity may belong to the same organisation, or to different organisations.
