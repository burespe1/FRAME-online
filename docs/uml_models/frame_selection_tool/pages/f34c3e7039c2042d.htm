List of Terminators


Definition of Terminator

A Terminator is the link between the System (or ITS) and the outside World. It provides a definition of what the System expects the outside World to do, the data it is expected to provide, and the data to be provided to it by the System. A Terminator may be a human entity, a system, or a physical entity from which data can be obtained such as the atmosphere, or road surface. Both human entities and systems may be part of Organisations or Public Authorities that contribute in some way to the provision of ITS services.
