Dataflow
mt_operator_inter-urban_lane_override


Description

It contains a request from the Operator to change the lane use setting on one or more parts of the urban road network. This may override the current speed setting, imposed by the operator, or automatically as part of a time of day dependent sequence of changes. The lane use setting may be for allocation of a lane to a specific type of vehicle, to close one or more lanes, or to change the direction of traffic flow in one or more lanes.
