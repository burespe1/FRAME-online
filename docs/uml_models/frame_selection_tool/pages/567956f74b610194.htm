Dataflow
td-current_commands_&_warnings


Description

It contains dynamic warnings for display to the Driver.  They may be about such things as traffic conditions, queues and their rates of propagation, speed limits, lane commands, road signs and general road information, plus warnings of the need to dip headlights, lane changing, safety behaviour, collision and emergency brake manoeuvres.
