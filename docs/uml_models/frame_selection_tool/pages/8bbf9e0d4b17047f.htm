Low Level Function
3.3.13 Output Demand Management Information

Overview

This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:

(1) The ability to take responsibility for the output of information to Drivers and/or Travellers as part of a demand management strategy.
(2) The ability for other functionality to provide details of what the information output should contain and to which group(s) of users the information should be output.

Functional Requirements

(a) when the demand management information data flow is received, send the appropriate parts of its contents to the driver and the traveller.
