Dataflow
mt.pshvs_vehicle_s&g_input


Description

It contains data about the length of time that the Driver of the Vehicle will see a red (stop) signal at a junction and shall enable the Vehicle System to decide whether or not it wants to shut down the Vehicle's engine for this period of time.
