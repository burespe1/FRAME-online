Dataflow
psef_global_emergency_progress_report


Description

This data flow is used within the Provide Safety and Emergency Facilities Area. It contains a report on the progress of activity by any and all Emergency Vehicles that are responding to emergency calls. It is provided to all Emergency Vehicles so that they can be aware of what else is happening and what (if anything) other vehicles may be doing to assist with responding to their particular emergency call.
