Terminator Fleet Operator
 Diagram   Definition    TabMemoryProcess

Overview

This Actor within the Operator Terminator shall represent a human entity that uses the facilities of the system to manage a fleet of freight carrying vehicles that are licensed to operate on the road network.  It shall be possible for the human entity that is the Fleet Operator to also fulfil the role of a Freight Operator.  The system may be in communication with more than one human entity that is a Fleet and/or Freight Operator.  Each entity may belong to the same fleet and/or freight management organisation, or to different organisations.
