Dataflow
tt.st-eCall_acknowledgement


Description

It contains the response that is being sent to a Traveller because they have made an "eCall" from outside of the Vehicle.  Typically each time it is sent it may contain one of the following : "OK we received your call and we process it" (first acknowledgement) or "ambulance will be there in 'X' minutes and police in 'Y' minutes" (full acknowledgement).
