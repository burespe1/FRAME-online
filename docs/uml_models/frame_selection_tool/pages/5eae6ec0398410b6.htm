Terminator Bridge/Tunnel Infrastructure
 Diagram   Definition    TabMemoryProcess

Overview

This Terminator shall represent the entities that can detect physical conditions of bridges and tunnels.  It shall consist of the following two Actors:
Bridge Structures
Tunnel Management System
The conditions that these Actors can detect shall comprise such things as the status of the bridge or tunnel infrastructure, atmospheric pollution levels on the bridge or in the tunnel, smoke (tunnels only) and weather conditions (bridges only) and be provided as data inputs to the System.
