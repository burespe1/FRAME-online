Dataflow
pshvs.mt_inter-urban_xfcd


Description

It contains extended floating car data (XFCD) from a Vehicle in the inter-urban traffic network.  This data will enable the reconstruction of the location, intended route (way points) and motion characteristics of the Vehicle and, together with that from other Vehicles, the traffic behaviour in their local geographic area.  Vehicle identity and status information such as ESP or ABS activities will - together with that from other vehicles - enable other data about traffic conditions to be determined, e.g. darkness (lights on), fog (fog lights on), rain (windscreen wipers active), Vehicle direction change (turn indicator use).
