This Terminator shall represent the movement of Vehicles at discrete points within the road network.  Functionality in the system shall be able to use its sensors to create data objects that indicate the presence of Vehicles.  The functionality shall be able to distinguish different types of Vehicle and the numbers of each type that are pass the discrete points. The sensors used by the functionality shall be able to detect Vehicles in different ways such as video, laser or infra-red images, magnetic signature or any other way in which the presence of a vehicle can be determined.<br/><br/>Mnemonic: trfc<br/>
