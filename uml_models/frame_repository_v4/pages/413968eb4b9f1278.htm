Dataflow
fesp.dvip-vehicle_data_for_urban_fcd_check


Description

It contains information about Vehicles that will enable the identities they provide through FCD/XFCD when passing through the urban road network to be matched with their actual physical identities, i.e. is the FCD/XFCD credible for the type of Vehicle that it appears to be.
