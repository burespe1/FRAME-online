Dataflow
to.po-un/loading_zone_outputs


Description

It contains all of the outputs to the Parking Zone Operator about the management of loading or unloading zones and holding areas for use when no zones are available at the requested locations, or dates/times, or for the requested Vehicle types or types of goods (e.g. hazardous goods).
