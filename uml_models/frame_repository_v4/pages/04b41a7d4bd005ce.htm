Dataflow
fd.hgvd-route_request_details


Description

It contains details of the route that is required for use by a Driver of a Vehicle carrying Hazardous Goods.  As a minimum the request shall include the destination, the type of Vehicle and the Hazardous Goods it is carrying and the required time of arrival.
