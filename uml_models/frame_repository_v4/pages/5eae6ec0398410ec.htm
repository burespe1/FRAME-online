Maintenance Organisation

This is a Terminator diagram which provides a description of the Terminator breakdown structure into Actors.

Overview

This terminator shall represent human entities or systems that are capable of carrying out maintenance activities.  It shall consist of the following Actors:
Public Transport Maintenance Organisation
Road Maintenance Organisation
These Actors shall be capable of being part of organisations able to carry out work to build and/or maintain a road network, carry out maintenance on equipment that is part of the System, or carry out maintenance on Public Transport related equipment that is used by the System.
