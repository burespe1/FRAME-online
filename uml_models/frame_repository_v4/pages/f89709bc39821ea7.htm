Low Level Function
1.1.2 Establish Contract Statistics

Overview

This Function shall be capable of providing the following facilities:

(1) The ability to process the different contracts made by the travellers to produce statistical information for the corresponding Electronic Fee Collection Operators, or Service Providers.

Functional Requirements

(a) process the different contracts (sent on a regular basis) to extract different kinds of information : e.g. number per service, chosen modes of payment, locations of contract set-up ...
(b) send the resulting statistics to the corresponding operators and/or service providers
