Dataflow
mt_urban_incident_warning_s&g_commands


Description

It contains details of the actual commands that are for the management of traffic at an incident that are to be displayed to drivers and other travellers using the urban road network.  Also included may be the identity and/or location of the urban devices on which the commands are to be displayed.  The display of these commands will override that of all other commands on the selected urban devices.
