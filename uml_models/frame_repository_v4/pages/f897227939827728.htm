Dataflow
fors.iutms-inter-urban_traffic_management_strategy


Description

It contains details of the new inter-urban traffic management strategy or special vehicle priority route that is just being implemented by a geographically adjacent (or relevant) inter-urban traffic management system.  The details will comprise such things as the affected junctions, method of control, actual junction timings, reason for change and previous strategy.
