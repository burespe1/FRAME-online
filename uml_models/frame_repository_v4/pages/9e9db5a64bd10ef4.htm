Dataflow
mffo.pscs_hazardous_goods_information


Description

It contains information that the type of hazardous goods that is being carried by a Freight Vehicle and is used in the planning for routes for this type of vehicle.  It may be sent either from the ground or from the on-board part of the Manage Freight and Fleet Operations Area.
