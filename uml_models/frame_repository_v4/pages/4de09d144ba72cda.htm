Dataflow
fd.tpd-vehicle_trip_plan_data


Description

It contains the parameters needed to plan a trip and as a minimum shall include the destination plus the desired arrival or departure times.  The identity of a predefined set of parameters that are frequently used may also be included, e.g. home, school, pick-up point, drop of point, and place of work.
