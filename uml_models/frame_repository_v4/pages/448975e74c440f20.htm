DFD 3.1.8 Provide Traffic Management for Bridges

This is a DFD diagram which provides a description of the function in the ITS environment

Overview

This High-level Function shall provide facilities for the control and management of road traffic usingh bridges.  It shall use inputs from equipment that monitors atmosheric conditions, weather forecasts and the bridge infrastructure to assess if ther are any weather conditions that pose a danger to Vehicles as well as their Drivers and passengers.  From this assessment the Function can provide warnings and other information to Drivers as they approach the bridges.  Facilities shall be provided by the Function to enable the Bridge Operator to monitor the actions taken as a result of the assessment process and to modify these actions or provide new ones.
