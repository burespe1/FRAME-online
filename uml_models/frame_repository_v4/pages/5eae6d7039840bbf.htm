Description

The Driver may be one of the types defined in the terminator description. These types will be identified by the names of the individual data flows from each Area. The data flow contains instructions for the Driver to take actions or responses to inputs from the Driver. These inputs will be requests for information, or actions for the Driver to take that will help to manage road traffic in an efficient manner.
