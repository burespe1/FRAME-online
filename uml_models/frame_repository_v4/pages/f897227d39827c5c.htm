Dataflow
tt-inter-urban_traffic_commands


Description

If contains output from the traffic management Functions that serve the inter-urban road network. This output will direct Travellers to take actions so that their progress will make best use of the inter-urban travel network. The output will apply to the Pedestrian (including those in wheelchairs or suffering from other disabilities) and Cyclist parts of the definition of the "dynamic" Traveller.
