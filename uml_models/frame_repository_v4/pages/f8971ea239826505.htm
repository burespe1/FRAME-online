mt.psle_inter-urban_enforcement_guidelines_PF


Description

It contains the recommendations established by the Manage Traffic area for the enforcement of rules in the inter-urban road network.  The data flow includes the following elements:
- date
- period of validity of guidelines
- area concerned
- types of fraud concerned
- for each type of fraud : level of fraud over which notification and / or prosecution is required, according to environmental conditions (weather, traffic conditions, ...)
