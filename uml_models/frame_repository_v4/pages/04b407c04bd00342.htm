fo.rno-hazardous_goods_vehilce_criteria_update


Description

It contains the criteria that are to be used in determining the route for Vehicles carrying Hazardous Goods.  The criteria may include geographic areas, particular roads or road types, bridges and tunnels, plus service area use and parking requirements that must be used or avoided for different forms of hazardous goods.
