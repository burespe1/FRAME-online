Dataflow
pshvs_vehicle_data_for_determining_speed_&_headway


Description

It contains data about the Vehicle such as identity, acceleration and braking characteristics, plus the weight when it is empty, the weight of goods being carried (if any), or the weight of any passengers (PT Vehicles only), or the weight of occupants and belongings (Private Car only), that will be used in determining the speed and headway that the Driver should use.
