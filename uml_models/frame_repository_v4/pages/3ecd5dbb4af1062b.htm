Dataflow
tt.p-urban_traffic_s&g_commands


Description

It contains the manifestation of the output of stop or go (s&g) commands to pedestrians (including those in wheelchairs or suffering from other disabilities) using the urban road network.  It will direct them to take actions so that their progress will make best use of the urban travel network.
