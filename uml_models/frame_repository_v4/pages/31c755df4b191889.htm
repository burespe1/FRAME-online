Data Store
4.6 Fare Card Data


Description of content

This Data Store shall be used within the Manage Public Transport Operations Area.  It shall contain details of the fare credit that is currently available for use by the PT Passenger.

The data in the Store shall be structured in the following way:
− amount of credit (number)
− date of last top up (date string)
− date of expiry (date string)
This Data Store shall represent a PT "fare card" in its simplest form.  As it goes with the PT passenger there is no need for any personal identification data.
