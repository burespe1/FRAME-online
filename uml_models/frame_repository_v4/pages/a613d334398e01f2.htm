Terminator Pre-Trip Traveller
 Diagram   Definition    TabMemoryProcess

Overview

This Actor within the Traveller Terminator represents a human entity that is using the facilities of the system to plan a journey.  It shall be possible for the Pre-trip Traveller to specify a journey that uses more than one mode of transport, although at least one of those modes should be road based.  It shall also be possible for the Pre-trip Traveller may also make any bookings necessary to carry out the planned journey.  The system may be in communication with more than one human entity each of which is a Pre-trip Traveller.
